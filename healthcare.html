Healthcare

Understand YTHS, appointment booking, and emergency support through simple step-by-step information.

What is YTHS

Student healthcare support

YTHS provides general health, mental wellbeing, and dental services for eligible students. It is a good first point of contact for many non-urgent student health needs.

Emergency contact

Urgent situations

Call 112 in an emergency. For non-urgent support, use student healthcare booking channels or local service advice lines.

1

Open service

Go to the YTHS website or app and choose your location or service type.

2

Select help type

Choose physical health, mental health, dental care, or guidance based on your needs.

3

Book appointment

Confirm your booking and prepare your ID, student details, and a short description of your issue.
